Knowledge Base - Content Articles

Unable to login to Customer Care Registry

Are you troubling with logging in to Customer Care Registry application, If yes, then don't get panic. At first, you must need to create an account in Customer Care Registry. For that, click this link to sign up with Customer Care Registry. Once filling the required details for the sign up process, you can be able to log in to Customer Care Registry without an error.

I forgot my Customer Care Registry account password

Have you forgotten your Customer Care Registry account password? If yes, then don't get panic. Click this link Forgot Password to enter into the forgot password page. In that page, you need to enter your existing Email ID. Once entering your Email ID, you'll be receiving the password via Email.

How to signup with Customer Care Registry as a new user?

In the landing page of the Customer Care Registry, you'll be able to view the sign up button. You need to click that button to sign up with Customer Care Registry. There you need to give the required details. And that's it, you have successfully created an account on Customer Care Registry.

How to Verify Customer Care Registry account?

Once making the sign up process, you'll be shown an OTP page. There you need to enter the OTP that is received via Email to verify your Customer Care Registry account.

How to send Canned Responses?

Once you logged in to your Agent account, in the response area, near textarea, you can be able to send the canned responses for the user.

How to change the status of the Ticket?

In the response area, at the bottom, near the description area, you can be able to view the dropdown box. In that, you can be able to fix the status of the ticket(open, pending, closed).

Is Live Chat is available in the Customer Care Registry?

Yes, Live Chat feature is available in Customer Care Registry. In that, you can able to chat and you'll receive instant responses with crystal clear quality.

How fast the live chat works?

While using Live chat, the user can able to send a query to the agent and the agent can able to send the responses instantly. It works 24/7.

How to raise a ticket?

Once you logged in to your Customer Care Registry account, you'll be redirected to the Customer dashboard. In that dashboard, you can raise a ticket by clicking the button named Raise a ticket.

How many channels does Customer Care Registry have?

Customer Care Registry contains three omni channels. They are Live chat, Email, and finally Knowledge base articles. Using these three channels, the user can get the support quickly.
