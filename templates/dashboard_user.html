 Oops! I forgot my account password!

Have you forgotten your Customer Care Registry account password? If yes, then don't get panic. Click this link Forgot Password to enter into the forgot password page. In that page, you need to enter your existing Email ID. Once entering your Email ID, you'll be receiving the password via Email.

 I can't able to login to Customer Care Registry!

Are you troubling with logging in to Customer Care Registry application, If yes, then don't get panic. At first, you must need to create an account in Customer Care Registry. For that, click this link to sign up with Customer Care Registry. Once filling the required details for the sign up process, you can be able to log in to Customer Care Registry without an error.

 How to signup with Customer Care Registry as a new user?

In the landing page of the Customer Care Registry, you'll be able to view the sign up button. You need to click that button to sign up with Customer Care Registry. There you need to give the required details. And that's it, you have successfully created an account on Customer Care Registry.

 How to Verify Customer Care Registry account?

Once making the sign up process, you'll be shown an OTP page. There you need to enter the OTP that is received via Email to verify your Customer Care Registry account.
